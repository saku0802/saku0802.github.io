SNS Management Tool "Twittersskills" Development

Project Overview

As Project Manager, I led a 10-person development team to create "Twittersskills," an SNS management tool.
We implemented features such as automated posting, follower analysis, and performance measurement utilizing the Twitter API.
This resulted in significant achievements, including adoption by over 100 companies within one month of release.

Technologies Used

  • Languages: Python, JavaScript
  • Frameworks: Django, React
  • Database: PostgreSQL
  • Others: AWS, Docker, CircleCI

Role

As Project Manager, I oversaw the entire project lifecycle, from requirements definition and design to development, testing, release, and operation.
My responsibilities included managing the engineering team, tracking progress, and ensuring quality control.

Achievements

  • Successfully met deadlines one month ahead of schedule
  • Achieved adoption by over 100 companies within one month of release
  • Achieved a customer satisfaction rate of 95%
